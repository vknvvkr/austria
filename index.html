AUSTRIA

Austria is a Central European country with an imperial history, baroque architecture, mountain villages and an alpine landscape. The official language here is German. The capital of Austria - the city of Vienna - is located on the Danube River. Popular attractions in Austria are the Bohemian Forest in the north of the country, Lake Traunsee and the vineyards located in the east.

The flag of Austria consists of three equal horizontal stripes of red(top), white and red. The eagle holds a hammer and sickle representing workers and peasants, personifying the middle class and urban residents. However, the eagle is not in all versions of flags.

Majestic mountains occupy more than half of modern Austria, forming an array in the center of the country. Deciduous and mixed forests dominate in the north of Austria. A rich flora and fauna has formed in the Southeast.

Vienna is the capital and largest city of Austria, located in the northeastern part of the country. This is a metropolis with a unique charm, energy and atmosphere. Vienna is the cultural capital of Europe, a surprisingly romantic and open city.
